About AgriBot

Empowering Farmers With
AI Technology

AgriBot is a smart agriculture Project that combines artificial intelligence, Crops Information Guidance, Farming Support, and government resources to help farmers make better decisions.

**This is only a Demo version for college project. We are planning to add more features in future.**

Our Mission

To help farmers with crop guidance, smart farming tools, and modern agricultural solutions.

Our Vision

To create a future where technology makes farming smarter, easier, and more productive.

WHY AGRiBOT?

Bridging the Gap in Indian Agriculture

The Problem

  • Late disease detection
  • Limited farming guidance
  • Difficulty accessing market information
  • Lack of awareness about government schemes

The Solution

  • AI-powered Chatbot for infomation gathering
  • Live crop prices from eNAM & mandis
  • Crop and market information
  • Government scheme portal with direct links
  • 24/7 AI chatbot & Kisan Call Center support
PLATFORM FEATURES

What Each Page Does

Complete ecosystem for modern farming needs

🌾 Crop Information

Use Case:

Farmers can search for crops based on season, soil type, and region. Get detailed growing instructions, fertilizer requirements, and expected yield.

📍 Season-wise recommendations | 📊 Price per quintal | 🌡️ Weather insights

🤖 AI Crop Analysis

Use Case:

Upload a photo of diseased crop/leaf. Our AI model detects the disease, identifies severity, and recommends organic/chemical treatment solutions instantly.

🧬 Disease detection | 💊 Treatment plan | 📈 Yield prediction

🏪 Tool Marketplace

Use Case:

Farmers can buy/sell agricultural tools, equipment, seeds, and organic medicines. Direct farmer-to-farmer and farmer-to-buyer transactions.

🛠️ Quality tools | 💊 Organic medicines | 🔒 Secure payments

🏛️ Government Resources

Use Case:

Direct access to PM-KISAN, eNAM, PMFBY crop insurance, Soil Health Card, and NABARD loans. One-click navigation to official portals.

💰 Schemes | 📊 Market | 🛡️ Insurance | 🌱 Soil testing

🌦️ Weather & Alerts

Use Case:

Live weather forecasts from IMD, crop-specific agromet advisories, and emergency alerts for cyclones, hailstorms, and pest outbreaks.

📡 IMD integration | 🌾 Crop advisories | ⚠️ Emergency alerts

📞 Farmer Support

Use Case:

24/7 access to Kisan Call Center (1800-180-1551), AI chatbot for instant queries, and expert consultation for farming problems.

📞 Toll-free helpline | 🤖 AI chatbot | 👨‍🌾 Expert advice
